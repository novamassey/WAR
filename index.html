RULES

DEAL

The play button will deal two cards, one for each player

PLAY

The player with the higher ranking cards wins the round and collects both cards

WAR

If both cards in play are of equal ranking, two cards will be dealt face down. Player must click the war button and the winner of this round collects all cards.

WIN

The player that has collected all 52 cards wins the game!
